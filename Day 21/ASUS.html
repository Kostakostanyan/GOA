About ASUS

Introduction

ASUS is a multinational computer hardware and electronics company headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan. The company is known for producing a wide range of products, including laptops, desktops, motherboards, graphics cards, monitors, networking equipment, and more.

History

ASUS was founded in 1989 by T.H. Tung, Ted Hsu, Wayne Hsieh, and M.T. Liao. The name ASUS is derived from Pegasus, the winged horse of Greek mythology. The company has grown to become one of the world's leading technology companies, known for its innovation and high-quality products.

Products

ASUS offers a diverse range of products catering to different consumer needs. Whether you're a gamer, content creator, or business professional, ASUS has a product lineup that includes ROG (Republic of Gamers) gaming products, TUF Gaming, ZenBook laptops, and more.
